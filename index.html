About Us

Computer and ICT Support is a team of Grade 12 Senior High School students from the ICT strand of Central Visayan Institute Foundation. As a growing ICT-focused group, we are dedicated to providing reliable support in technology-related tasks such as troubleshooting, technical assistance, and digital resource management. Our goal is to bridge classroom learning with real-world application, ensuring efficiency, teamwork, and professionalism in every project we handle. We strive to deliver solutions that not only meet immediate needs but also prepare us for future roles in the ICT industry.

Mission & Vision

Mission

We envision ourselves as future ICT professionals who empower schools and organizations through reliable technical support and digital solutions. At CVIF, we aspire to leave a lasting impact by fostering a culture of innovation, collaboration, and excellence in the use of technology for education.

Vision

We envision CVIF as a digitally-empowered learning community where technology supports both teaching and administration. Through our computerized reservation system, we aim to create a legacy project that will become a model for efficiency, innovation, and collaboration in education.

Goals

1

Digitalize the current manual reservation system while retaining its core functions and structure.

2

Ensure teachers remain as administrators of the system, with control over approvals, monitoring, and updates.

3

Create a secure platform that prevents unauthorized reservations and system misuse.

4

Develop a system accessible to all teachers using simple and intuitive features.

5

Maintain reservation history digitally for easier tracking, accountability, and transparency.

6

Provide training and orientation for teachers to transition smoothly from the manual system to the computerized one.

7

Leave a legacy project that future CVIF ICT immersion students can enhance, upgrade, and expand.
